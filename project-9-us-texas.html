Choices:

  1. Learn about Texas vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the Texas Public Health site.
  3. Search for online Texas vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore Texas birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Learn about the Texas Birth Index in this Ancestry video.
  6. Explore Texas birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.

Goal 12: Get help with your Texas research.


Choices:

A. Use Facebook pages to get answers to your questions.

  1. Visit the Texas Genealogy Network page on Facebook.
  2. Visit the Texas Genealogy page on Facebook.
  3. Visit the Texas State Genealogical Society page on Facebook.
  4. See the Genealogy on Facebook list of Facebook groups dedicated to helping with research for Texas.

B. Join a mailing list or message board for Texas research.

  1. Join the RootsChat forum and post questions about Texas research.
  2. Search the RootsWeb site for Texas research mailing lists.
  3. Explore the Texas research mailing lists on Cyndi's List.
  4. Use the RootsWeb message boards to read or post questions about Texas research.

C. Learn about Texas genealogy societies.

  1. Learn about genealogy societies in Texas in this FamilySearch Wiki article.
  2. Learn about genealogy societies in Texas in this FamilySearch Wiki article.
