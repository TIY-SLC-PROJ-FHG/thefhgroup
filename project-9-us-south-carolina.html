Goal 3: Learn about South Carolina vital records.


Choices:

A. Learn about South Carolina vital records.

  1. Learn about South Carolina vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the South Carolina Public Health site (Public Records tab).
  3. Search for online South Carolina vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore South Carolina birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore South Carolina birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.
  6. Get help with adoption topics on the South Carolina Adoption Reunion Registry Facebook page.

B. Explore South Carolina marriage and death records.

  1. Explore links for South Carolina marriage records.
  2. Explore the Ancestors at Rest site for South Carolina death records.
  3. Explore the ObituariesHelp site for South Carolina obituaries.
  4. Explore South Carolina death records, indexes, and obituaries.
