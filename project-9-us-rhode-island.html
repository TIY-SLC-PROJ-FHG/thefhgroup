  1. Learn about Rhode Island vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the Rhode Island Public Health site.
  3. Search for online Rhode Island vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore Rhode Island birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore Rhode Island birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.
  6. Get help with adoption topics on the Rhode Island Adoptee Resource Group Facebook page.
