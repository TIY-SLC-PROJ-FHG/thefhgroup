Goal 3: Learn about South Dakota vital records.


Choices:

A. Learn about South Dakota vital records.

  1. Learn about South Dakota vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the South Dakota Public Health site.
  3. Search for online South Dakota vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore South Dakota birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore South Dakota birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.

B. Explore South Dakota marriage and death records.

  1. Explore links for South Dakota marriage records.

  3. Explore the ObituariesHelp site for South Dakota obituaries.
  4. Explore South Dakota death records, indexes, and obituaries.
