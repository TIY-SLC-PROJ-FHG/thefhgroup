Goal 3: Learn about Wyoming vital records.


Choices:

A. Learn about Wyoming vital records.

  1. Learn about Wyoming vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the Wyoming Public Health site.
  3. Search for online Wyoming vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore Wyoming birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore Wyoming birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.

B. Explore Wyoming marriage and death records.

  1. Explore links for Wyoming marriage records.
  2. Learn about Wyoming divorce records in this article.
  3. Explore the Ancestors at Rest site for Wyoming death records.
  4. Explore the ObituariesHelp site for Wyoming obituaries.
  5. Explore Wyoming death records, indexes, and obituaries.

Goal 12: Get help with your Wyoming research.


Choices:

A. Use Facebook pages to get answers to your questions.

  1. Visit the Wyoming Genealogy Network page on Facebook.
  2. Visit the Wyoming Genealogy page on Facebook.
  3. Visit the Wyoming Genealogical Society page on Facebook.
  4. See the Genealogy on Facebook list of Facebook groups dedicated to helping with research for Wyoming.

B. Join a mailing list or message board for Wyoming research.

  1. Join the RootsChat forum and post questions about Wyoming research.
  2. Search the RootsWeb site for Wyoming research mailing lists.
  3. Explore the Wyoming research mailing lists on Cyndi's List.
  4. Use the RootsWeb message boards to read or post questions about Wyoming research.

C. Learn about Wyoming genealogy societies.

  1. Learn about genealogy societies in Wyoming in this FamilySearch Wiki article.
  2. Explore Wyoming genealogy society sites on Cyndi's list.
