Goal 3: Learn about Utah vital records.


Choices:

A. Learn about Utah vital records.

  1. Learn about Utah vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the Utah Public Health site.
  3. Search for online Utah vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore Utah birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore Utah birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.
  6. Get help with adoption topics on the Utah Adoption Search and Reunion Facebook page.

  1. Explore links for Utah marriage records.
